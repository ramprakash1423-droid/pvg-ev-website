Company

PVG EV is Prime Ventures Global’s clean mobility charging platform.

We combine mobile charging, site-based charging infrastructure, digital energy systems and operating support into one EV charging ecosystem.

Built for practical EV adoption.

PVG EV supports EV users and businesses with charging models that match real parking, routing and uptime needs. The platform is designed for residential communities, corporate destinations, fleet depots, highways and commercial parking locations.

  • Mobile charging for doorstep and urgent charging use cases.
  • Fleet-ready infrastructure for taxis, delivery vehicles and logistics.
  • Commercial station deployment for high-value public destinations.
  • Energy management systems for visibility, reporting and optimization.

Prime Ventures Global

PVG EV is positioned to support India’s EV transition with scalable clean mobility infrastructure and service-led charging operations.
