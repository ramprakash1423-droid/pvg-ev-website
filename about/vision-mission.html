Vision & Mission

Making EV charging accessible, intelligent and sustainable.

PVG EV aims to remove charging friction by bringing charging closer to customers and making infrastructure easier to operate.

Vision

To become a trusted clean mobility infrastructure company that enables EV adoption across cities, fleets, residential communities and commercial corridors.

Mission

To deploy reliable charging products, mobile charging services and intelligent energy systems that make EV charging simple to access and manage.

Commitment

To build with reliability, transparency, sustainable energy focus and customer-first charging operations.
