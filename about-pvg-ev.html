About PVG-EV

About PVG-EV

PVG-EV is the electric-mobility and charging-solutions initiative of Prime Ventures Global.

Company

Introducing practical EV-charging solutions for South India.

The business has been created to identify, introduce and scale practical EV-charging solutions in markets where customers, fleets and businesses require greater flexibility and improved charging access.

PVG-EV’s initial focus is the introduction of mobile EV charging in Chennai and Tamil Nadu through its collaboration with Setrans.

The longer-term vision is to build a connected portfolio of mobile, fleet, commercial and residential EV-charging solutions across South India.

MissionTo make EV charging more accessible, flexible and commercially practical for customers, fleets and businesses.
VisionTo become a trusted South Indian mobility-infrastructure partner recognised for introducing innovative charging solutions and building strong technology and operating partnerships.

Market and Sustainability Context

Focused on practical adoption of cleaner mobility.

Tamil Nadu is one of India’s most important automotive, industrial and electric-mobility markets.

As EV adoption expands across Chennai and other major urban and industrial centres, flexible charging solutions will be increasingly important for fleet operators, businesses, residential communities and mobility-service providers.
