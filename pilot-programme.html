Chennai Mobile EV Charging Pilot

PVG-EV is preparing a structured Chennai pilot to assess demand, vehicle compatibility, operating locations and deployment feasibility using Setrans mobile charging technology.

What the Pilot Will Evaluate

Operational evidence before wider deployment.

  • Customer demand
  • Vehicle and connector compatibility
  • Charging patterns
  • Fleet operating routes
  • Deployment locations
  • Operating workflow
  • Safety and site conditions
  • Commercial feasibility
  • Support and response process
  • Data required for future scale-up
