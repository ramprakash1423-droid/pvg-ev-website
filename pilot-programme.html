Pilot Programme

Chennai Mobile EV Charging Pilot

PVG-EV is preparing the deployment of a Setrans Mobile Charging Station in Chennai as the first phase of its South India market-development programme.

Pilot Status

Current status: Pilot preparation and partner registration.

The pilot will assess customer demand, fleet requirements, charging patterns, operating routes, response times and commercial models. The network should not be described as live until operational readiness is confirmed.

Insights from the pilot will support the introduction of higher-capacity mobile charging units and wider deployment across Tamil Nadu and other South Indian markets.

Project-status timeline

  1. Product development / readiness planning
  2. Testing and certification
  3. Chennai deployment
  4. Pilot customer onboarding
  5. Controlled service launch
  6. Regional expansion

Objectives

What the Chennai pilot will validate

Validate market demand

Understand interest from EV owners, fleets, businesses and mobility operators.

Engage fleet and commercial customers

Work with early customers to assess practical use cases.

Assess routes and response times

Study operating routes, dispatch flow and service windows.

Test booking and service processes

Evaluate customer registration, service planning and support workflows.

Evaluate charging patterns

Review utilisation, session needs and charging behaviour.

Develop commercial models

Use pilot learnings to shape scalable customer and fleet models.

Build expansion roadmap

Support future Tamil Nadu and South India expansion planning.
