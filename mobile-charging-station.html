Product Overview

What is a Mobile Charging Station?

A Mobile Charging Station is an EV-charging system integrated into a commercial vehicle. It can be deployed to customer, fleet or commercial locations to provide charging support without requiring the EV to travel to a conventional fixed charging station.

The solution is designed to provide planned, on-demand and operational charging support at locations where permanent charging infrastructure may be unavailable, insufficient or impractical.

Fleet operating base

Fleet charging support

  • Taxi, delivery and commercial fleet operating bases
  • Vehicle compatibility and location access review

Commercial site

Commercial-site charging

Temporary mobile charging support may be evaluated for offices, warehouses, project sites and event locations where permanent charging infrastructure is unavailable.

  • Offices, warehouses, project sites and event venues
  • Temporary connection planning with safe vehicle movement
  • Feasibility review before pilot scheduling
