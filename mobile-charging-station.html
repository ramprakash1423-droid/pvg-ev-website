What is a Mobile Charging Station?

A Mobile Charging Station is an EV-charging system integrated into a commercial vehicle. It can be deployed to customer, fleet or commercial locations to provide charging support without requiring the EV to travel to a conventional fixed charging station.

FAQ

Mobile Charging Station questions

Is the service live?

PVG-EV is preparing the Chennai pilot. The network should not be described as live until operational readiness is confirmed.

Who owns the technology role?

Setrans is the technology and engineering partner. PVG-EV is the local market-development, deployment and operating partner.

Can technical values be published?

No. Charging capacity, connector details and other technical values should be published only after Setrans approval.
