PVG-EV × Setrans

PVG-EV and Setrans: Powering Mobile Charging in South India

PVG-EV and Setrans have come together to introduce a practical new mobile EV-charging model for Tamil Nadu and the wider South Indian market.

Collaboration Model

Engineering capability meets local market development.

The collaboration combines Setrans’ engineering and Mobile Charging Station capability with PVG-EV’s local market knowledge, customer development, operating model and regional-growth strategy.

Setrans’ roleTechnology, engineering and manufacturing partner

Setrans leads the engineering, product development, manufacturing and technical integration of the Mobile Charging Station. Its responsibilities include product design, vehicle integration, charging-system configuration, technical documentation, product testing and technical support, subject to the agreed partnership framework.

PVG-EV’s roleMarket development and operating partner

PVG-EV leads market development, pilot deployment, customer engagement, partnerships, fleet onboarding, service operations, local commercial development and regional expansion.

Shared Objective

A safe, commercially viable and scalable mobile EV-charging ecosystem.

The shared objective is to build a safe, commercially viable and scalable mobile EV-charging ecosystem that supports customers while complementing the growth of fixed charging infrastructure.

Clear technology ownership

Setrans is positioned as technology and engineering partner.

Strong local operating capability

PVG-EV leads local market development and service operations.

Customer-focused deployment

Pilot deployment is shaped around real customer and fleet requirements.

Safe and compliant operations

Operations are subject to applicable safety and operating procedures.

Pilot-led market development

Expansion follows pilot results, utilisation and customer demand.

Transparent performance measurement

Pilot insights will guide operating routes, response times and commercial models.

Scalable regional expansion

The long-term focus is Tamil Nadu and wider South India.

Build the Collaboration Network
