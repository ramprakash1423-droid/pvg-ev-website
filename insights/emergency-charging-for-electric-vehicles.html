Emergency Charging for Electric Vehicles

How mobile charging can support energy assistance before vehicles reach fixed chargers.

Author: PVG-EV Editorial Team · Publication date: July 12, 2026

Overview

How mobile charging can support energy assistance before vehicles reach fixed chargers. This article is intended as a factual guide for EV owners, fleets, businesses and property operators evaluating flexible charging models in Chennai, Tamil Nadu and South India.

Why it matters

EV adoption depends on practical charging access. Fixed infrastructure remains essential, but mobile charging can support locations, vehicles and operating conditions where flexible capacity is required.

PVG-EV context

PVG-EV is preparing a Chennai pilot using Setrans Mobile Charging Station technology. Pilot insights will help assess customer demand, fleet requirements, charging patterns, response times and potential commercial models.
