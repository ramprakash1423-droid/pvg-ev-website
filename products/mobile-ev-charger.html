Mobile EV Charger

A flexible charging vehicle designed to bring EV charging directly to cars, fleets, apartments, offices, highways and commercial parking spaces.

Charging that reaches the vehicle.

The PVG EV mobile charger is built for locations where fixed charging is unavailable, fully occupied or not practical. It supports emergency charging, scheduled doorstep requests and fleet support routes.

  • Doorstep charging requests for EV owners.
  • Emergency support for stranded or low-battery vehicles.
  • Fleet top-up routes for high-utilization vehicles.
  • Commercial parking and event charging operations.

Best for

EV owners, apartments, offices, fleet yards, highways, malls and hotels.
