How Mobile Charging Works

From request to charging review.

PVG-EV reviews each requirement before confirming service availability, location feasibility and pilot suitability.

01

Request

Customers, fleet operators or businesses submit a charging requirement through the PVG-EV platform, website or service team.

02

Assess

PVG-EV reviews the vehicle type, charging requirement, location, connector compatibility and requested service time.

03

Schedule

The Mobile Charging Station is scheduled and deployed to the agreed location, subject to operational availability.

04

Charge

The vehicle is connected and charged in accordance with applicable operating and safety procedures.

Key Customer Benefits

Charging support without making the customer search for a fixed station.

Mobile charging can help bridge access gaps for EV owners, fleets and businesses while fixed infrastructure continues to develop.

Charging delivered closer to the vehicle

Support planned or urgent charging needs without requiring the EV to travel to a fixed station.

Reduced dependence on fixed locations

Complement public and depot charging where access is limited, congested or still developing.

Flexible deployment for fleets and businesses

Support operating bases, parking locations, temporary demand and high-use fleet routes.

Emergency and planned charging support

Enable both scheduled charging use cases and energy assistance requirements.
