FAQ

Answers for charging customers and infrastructure buyers.

What is mobile EV charging?

Mobile EV charging brings charging equipment closer to the parked vehicle, helping customers and fleets top up without depending only on fixed public chargers.

How does doorstep EV charging work?

A charging request is scheduled, a PVG EV mobile unit reaches the location, the vehicle is connected safely, and the charging session is monitored until completion.

Can PVG EV support fleets?

Yes. PVG EV supports depot charging, route-based planning, backup charging, monitoring and uptime workflows for high-usage EV fleets.

Can apartments use PVG EV chargers?

Yes. PVG EV can support apartment parking through residential chargers, shared charging planning and mobile charging support.

Do you provide energy management?

Yes. PVG EV provides smart monitoring concepts for energy usage, load balancing, utilization, diagnostics and fleet dashboards.

How can businesses work with PVG EV?

Businesses can contact PVG EV to discuss charging infrastructure, mobile charging, fleet projects, commercial sites and technology integration.
